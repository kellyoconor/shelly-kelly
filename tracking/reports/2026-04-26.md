- **Alignment:** letting the foot calm down instead of forcing a fake-gritty comeback too early

### 🏃‍♀️ Body
The body story this week was not about volume. It was about cost.

That is a big clue. Fitness did not vanish in ten days. Rhythm did. Same-ish pace, higher physiological price.

So the most honest read is: the engine is still there, but the ease is not.

The pattern was clearer than any one metric:

- late week = rougher sleep, rougher readiness, then a return run that felt harder than the pace said it should

One more interesting note: the system kept surfacing that your **HRV had been improving over the past month**, even if this week itself did not feel smooth. That is important. It suggests the deeper recovery trend may be better than the day-to-day mood of the week. In other words: your body may be rebuilding before your confidence fully catches up.

This was a “re-entry costs more than expected” week. Not a failure week.
