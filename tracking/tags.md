# Tag Schema

## Domains
- `running` — runs, training, race stuff
- `fitness` — SoulCycle, strength, cross-training
- `sleep` — Oura scores, sleep patterns
- `food` — meals, nutrition, patterns
- `social` — friends, going out, drinking
- `work` — job stress, wins, events
- `style` — outfits, piercings, shopping
- `mood` — anxiety, energy, motivation
- `travel` — trips, planning
- `dating` — when she's ready
- `building` — Shelly/AI/tech stuff

## Energy Levels
- `high` — motivated, productive, excited
- `good` — normal, steady
- `low` — tired, sluggish, off
- `crashed` — hungover, anxious, depleted

## Triggers (what causes shifts)
- `alcohol` → next day energy crash, Oura tanks
- `dehydration` → headaches, low energy
- `poor_sleep` → anxiety, comfort food, low motivation
- `rest_day` → recovery, better scores next day
- `good_sleep` → better food choices, higher energy
- `social_high` → stays out late, worth it emotionally
- `work_stress` → comfort food, late nights

## Recurring Patterns
(Updated as spotted)
- Sleep < 70 → bad next day
- Alcohol → readiness drops 15-20 points next day
- Rest days → readiness rebounds 15-20 points
- Stressed → same comfort lunch (breaded chicken wrap)
- Energized → healthier choices (chicken salad, sushi)
- Late nights cluster: excitement OR social, rarely just insomnia
