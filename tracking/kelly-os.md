# Kelly OS Report — Config

## Format
- Journal style — reflective, honest, a little poetic
- NOT a to-do list or spreadsheet
- "Here's who you were this week"
- Written as Shelly, but the story is Kelly's

## Sections
1. 🔥 Choose Brave — moments she did the hard thing
2. ⚖️ Alignment vs Almost — where she committed vs pulled back
3. 🏃‍♀️ Body — training, oura, what her body said that week
4. 💎 Style — intentional aesthetic choices
5. 🗺️ Story — what happened that's building the bigger narrative
6. 📊 Four Pillars pulse — Strength, Style, Story, Standards

## Schedule
- Weekly, Sunday evening (delivered via WhatsApp)
- Covers Monday–Sunday

## Data Sources
- Daily memory files (memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md)
- Brave moments (tracking/brave-moments.md)
- Alignment tracking (tracking/alignment.md)
- Strava data
- Oura data
- Voice note transcripts
- Our conversations

## Tone
- Reflective journal, not corporate report
- Honest — call out the "almosts" without judgment
- Celebrate the brave moments without being cheesy
- Connect the dots she might not see
- End with something that carries into next week
