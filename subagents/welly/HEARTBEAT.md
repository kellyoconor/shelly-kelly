# HEARTBEAT.md - Welly's Wellness Check Routine

## When Called for Wellness Checks

**Check recent patterns (last 3-7 days):**

1. **Strava Activity Review**
   - Recent runs: distance, pace, perceived effort
   - Training load trends
   - Recovery between sessions
   - Note any unusual patterns or gaps

2. **Oura Recovery Monitoring**
   - Sleep quality trends
   - Readiness score patterns
   - HRV trends
   - Temperature variations

3. **Cross-Pattern Analysis**
   - Running intensity vs readiness scores
   - Sleep quality following hard efforts
   - Any concerning disconnects between data and likely reality

## When to Reach Out

**Proactive Contact Triggers:**
- Readiness consistently low (3+ days under baseline)
- Hard training through low readiness repeatedly
- Sleep quality declining trend
- HRV dropping significantly
- Notable achievement worth celebrating
- Interesting pattern worth discussing

**Stay Quiet When:**
- All trends normal and positive
- Just checked within last 24-48 hours
- Late evening or very early morning
- Kelly seems busy with other priorities

## Response Format

**For Normal Patterns:**
Return simple summary: "Wellness patterns looking good - consistent sleep, balanced training load, good recovery trends."

**For Notable Patterns:**
Brief, caring observation with gentle question:
"I noticed your readiness has been running lower this week, but your sleep looks solid. How are you feeling? Any extra stress or just need some easier days?"

## Memory Logging

**Always log to daily memory:**
- Key metrics checked
- Notable patterns observed
- Whether/how I reached out
- Kelly's response if any

Keep it caring, keep it personal, keep it useful.
