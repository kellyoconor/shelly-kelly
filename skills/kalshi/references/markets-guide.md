## Market Analysis Tips

### Reading Market Odds
- Prices are in cents (1-99¢)
- 75¢ = 75% implied probability
- Look for markets with high volume for better liquidity
- Check bid-ask spreads for trading costs

### Risk Management
- Never risk more than you can afford to lose
- Diversify across multiple markets
- Consider correlation between related markets
- Set stop-losses on volatile positions

### Information Edge
- Follow news closely for market-moving events
- Understand base rates and historical precedents
- Look for mispriced markets based on your knowledge
- Consider timing of resolution dates

## Common Strategies

### Value Betting
- Find markets where you believe the true probability differs from market price
- Focus on areas of expertise where you have information advantages
- Be patient - good opportunities may be rare

### Arbitrage
- Look for related markets with inconsistent pricing
- Consider "Yes" and "No" prices that don't sum to 100¢
- Account for transaction costs and timing

### Trend Following
- Monitor momentum in market prices
- Watch for news catalysts that might continue trends
- Be aware of potential reversals at extreme prices

## Market Resolution

### Understanding Resolution
- Markets resolve to either "Yes" (100¢) or "No" (0¢)
- Resolution criteria are specified in market rules
- Kalshi has final authority on resolution decisions
- Appeals process exists for disputed resolutions

### Key Dates
- Always check market close and resolution dates
- Some markets may resolve early if outcome is certain
- Weekend and holiday market hours may vary
- Time zones matter for event-based markets
